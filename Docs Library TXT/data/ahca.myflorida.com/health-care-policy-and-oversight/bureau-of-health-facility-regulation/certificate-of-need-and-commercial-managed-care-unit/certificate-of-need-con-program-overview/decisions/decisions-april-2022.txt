Challenges should be noted on this website within two months of the initial Agency decision. Recommended Orders and Agency Final Orders can be viewed on the DOAH websites by entering the Case Number at: https://www.doah.state.fl.us/ALJ/Final Agency action may be appealed to the District Court of Appeals (DCA).
