Forensic Toxicology Laboratories











 Forensic Toxicology LaboratoriesForensic toxicology laboratories are specialized laboratories licensed by the State of Florida for the purpose of performing drug testing for employers within the state. These laboratories may be certified through the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA), a division within the Department of Health and Human Services of the Federal Government.All employers within the State that wish to participate in the "Drug-Free Workplace Program", sponsored through the Department of Worker's Compensation, must contract with a licensed forensic toxicology Laboratory to implement their program.Notices/Updates Statute/Rule AuthorityChapter 112, Part I, Florida StatuteChapter 440.102, Florida StatuteChapter 59A-24, Florida Administrative CodeChapter 408, Part II, Florida StatuteChapter 59A-35, Florida Administrative CodeApplications/FormsApplication form (Initial, Renewal, Change of Ownership, and Change During Licensure Period)Resources
