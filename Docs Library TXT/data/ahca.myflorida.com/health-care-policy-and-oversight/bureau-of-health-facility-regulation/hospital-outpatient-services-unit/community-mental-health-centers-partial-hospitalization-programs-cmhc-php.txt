Community Mental Health Centers / Partial Hospitalization Programs (CMHC/PHP)This is a Medicare certification program for a partial hospitalization program for community mental health providers. These programs include services for mental illness and substance abuse disorders.Notices/UpdatesNone at this timeLicense/Certification RequirementsCertification requires submission of an application and other documentation. The Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) contracts with the state to perform site-visits. Also, some community mental health providers or partial hospitalization programs may require licensure as a "health care clinic". Additional information on licensure as a health care clinic can be obtained from the Agency's Health Care Clinic Unit.Regulations/GuidanceFlorida has outpatient licensure requirements only for substance abuse services and health care clinics. The Department of Children and Families regulates substance abuse services, and the Agency's Health Care Clinic Unit licenses health care clinics. Unless required by the "Health Care Clinic Act", there are no state licensing requirements imposed by the Agency.42 CFR, Part 410, Subpart A, Sections 410.1-.542 CFR, Part 485, Subpart J, Sections 485.900-.920CMS State Operations Manual[  ](See Sections 2250-2252P)
