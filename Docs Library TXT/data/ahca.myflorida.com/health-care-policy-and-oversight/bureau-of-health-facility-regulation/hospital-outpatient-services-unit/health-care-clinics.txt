Health Care ClinicsThe Hospital & Outpatient Services Unit licenses entities where health care services are provided to individuals and which tender charges for reimbursement for such services, including a mobile clinic and a portable equipment provider.Notices/UpdatesOnline Applications – In addition to submitting license renewal applications online, licensees may also submit changes during the licensure period. Changes to the fictitious name, address, personnel, and controlling interest of less than 51% can be submitted by going to the online portal and creating a new application.  Contact the licensure unit for additional information.
