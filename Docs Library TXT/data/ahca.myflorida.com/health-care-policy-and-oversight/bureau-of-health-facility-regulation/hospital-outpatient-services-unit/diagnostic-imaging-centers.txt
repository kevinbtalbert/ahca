Diagnostic Imaging Centers











Diagnostic Imaging CentersIn accordance with Section 456.053, Florida Statutes, all group practices and sole providers are required to report annually to the Agency for Health Care Administration if they accept outside referrals for diagnostic imaging services. The Legislature lists the following as diagnostic imaging services:Magnetic resonance imagingNuclear medicineAngiographyArteriographyPosition emission tomographyDigital vascular imagingBronchographyLymphangiographySplenographyUltrasoundEEGEKGNerve conduction studiesEvoked potentialsComputed tomographyNote: Effective March 1, 2004, all health care clinics as specified in the "Health Care Clinic Act," ss. 400.990-.995, F.S., are required to be licensed by the Agency for Health Care Administration. Additional information on health care clinic licensure or exemption may be obtained in the Health Care Clinic section of the Agency web site.Notices/UpdatesNone at this timeReporting Requirement
