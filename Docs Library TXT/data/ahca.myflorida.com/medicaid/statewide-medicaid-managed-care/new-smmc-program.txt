New SMMC Program











The Agency for Health Care Administration (Agency) has posted  Invitations to Negotiate (ITN) related to the Statewide Medicaid Managed Care (SMMC) program re-procurement for the Managed Medical Assistance (MMA) and Long-Term Care (LTC) Programs (AHCA ITN 22/23 010) and the Statewide Medicaid Prepaid Dental Program (AHCA ITN 007-23/24). This is the second re-procurement since the program began in 2013, and the Agency’s goal is to be as transparent as possible by making information publicly available and engaging with stakeholders.Information about the re-procurement will be distributed through multiple platforms including posting information on this website. To view ITN-related information and documents, click on the Procurement Updates icon below.  You can also sign-up to receive updates throughout the re-procurement process by clicking on the Sign Up for Medicaid Alerts button at the bottom of this page.Click on the individual icons below for more information about the re-procurement and the 2025 SMMC Program.
