IDD Pilot Program











COMING SOONNew Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities Managed Care Pilot 



The Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities Pilot Program is an additional pathway providing supports and services for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities in Hardee, Highlands, Hillsborough, Manatee, Miami-Dade, Monroe and Polk counties.The Agency will contract with Florida Community Care (FCC) health plan for both Regions 6 (D) and 11 (I).It is anticipated that the pilot will be implemented in Summer 2024. 



Check this site often for updates.














Services Included in the Pilot:Managed Medical Assistance servicesLong-Term Care servicesIntellectual and Developmental Disabilities servicesExpanded Benefits








Who is Eligible for the Pilot:Individuals who are 18 years or older in a pre-enrollment category at APD in the counties listed aboveIndividuals who are eligible for MedicaidEnrollment in the Pilot is limited
