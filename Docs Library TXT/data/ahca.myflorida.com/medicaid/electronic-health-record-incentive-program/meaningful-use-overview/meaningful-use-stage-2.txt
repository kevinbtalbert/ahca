Meaningful Use: Stage 2On August 23, 2012, CMS released the State 2 Meaningful Use Final Rule. Through the Stage 2 requirements of the EHR Incentive Programs, CMS expanded meaningful use of certified EHR technology. The rule consolidated several existing Stage 1 objectives and added new objectives for Stage 2. There will be 20 measures for EPs (17 core and 3 of 6 menu) in Stage 2.Stage 2 is effective January 1, 2014 for EPs and all providers attesting to Meaningful Use will have a 90 day reporting period in 2014 regardless of the EP’s Meaningful Use stage.For more information about the final rule see the CMS Stage 2 Final Rule Fact Sheet[  ].
