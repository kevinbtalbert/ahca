Five-Year  Extension Request (1/1/18 – 12/31/22): Florida’s Medicaid’s 1115 Family  Planning Waiver (Project Number 11-W-00135/4), Public Notice Document

The State is seeking  federal authority to extend Florida Medicaid’s 1115 Family Planning Waiver  (Project Number 11-W-00135/4) for the period January 1, 2018 through December  31, 2022.  The Family Planning Waiver operates statewide and provides  family planning services to women between the ages of 14 and 55 years who have  lost Florida Medicaid eligibility and meet the financial eligibility criteria.
 Public Comment Period: May  1, 2017 - May 30, 2017  
The State is conducting a  30-day public notice and comment period to solicit meaningful input from the  public on the waiver extension request. The State will consider all public  comments received regarding the proposed waiver extension. The 30-day  public comment period will be held from May 1, 2017 through May 30, 2017,  during which the State will hold two public meetings.
