Family Planning Extension 2023-28











The Agency for Health Care Administration (Agency) is conducting a 30-day public comment period to solicit meaningful input from the public on the proposed 5-year extension request for Florida’s 1115 Family Planning Waiver (Project Number 11-W-00135/4). The Family Planning Waiver operates statewide and provides family planning services to women between the ages of 14 and 55 years who have lost Florida Medicaid eligibility and meet the financial eligibility criteria.The 30-day public comment period will be held from March 28, 2023 through April 26, 2023, during which the Agency will hold two public meetings. The meetings will provide attendees a description of the extension request and allow time for public comments. To view a comprehensive description of the waiver extension request, please click on the following link:
