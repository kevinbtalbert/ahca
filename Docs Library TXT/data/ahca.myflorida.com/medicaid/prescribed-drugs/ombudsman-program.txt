Ombudsman ProgramAs a result of a settlement agreement in the Hernandez vs. Medows lawsuit, effective May 14, 2004, Florida Medicaid began providing the services of an Ombudsman to facilitate the timely resolution of claim reimbursement rejections, when the problems cannot be resolved through self-help by the recipient or intervention by the pharmacy or the prescriber.Information Pamphlets available in
