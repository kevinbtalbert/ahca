Florida HCBS Distribution Methodology











HCBS Funding Application InstructionsSubmit a QuestionFrequently Asked QuestionsDistribution MethodologyHCBS Spending PlanFlorida HCBS Distribution MethodologyFor the One-Time Provider Stipend and One-Time Provider Retention payments, final award amounts to qualified providers will be determined following the application period. The Agency has identified qualified providers for the provider stipend and retention payments as providers who:Are enrolled in Florida Medicaid as Provider Enrollment Types (PT) PT 14 (Assistive Care Services), 65 (Home Health), 67 (Home and Community Based Service) and 91 (Case Management Agency), who provide services to recipients enrolled in an HCBS waiver;Have had paid fee-for-service (FFS) claims and/or managed care paid encounter claims for services through an HCBS waiver for dates of services during State Fiscal Year 2020-2021 (July 1, 2020 through June 30, 2021);Are currently enrolled and be in good standing with Florida Medicaid.
