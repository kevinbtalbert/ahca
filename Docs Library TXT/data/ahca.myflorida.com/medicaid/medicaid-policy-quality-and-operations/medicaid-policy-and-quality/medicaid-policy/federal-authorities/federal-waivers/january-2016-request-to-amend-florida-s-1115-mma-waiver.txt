January 2016 - Request to Amend Florida’s 1115 MMA Waiver











Federal AuthoritiesManaged Medical AssistanceJanuary 2016 - Request to Amend Florida’s 1115 MMA Waiver (Project Number 11-W-002064) and Public InputThe State is seeking federal authority to amend Florida’s 1115 Managed Medical Assistance (MMA) Waiver (Project Number 11-W-00206/4) to  amend the language related to the Hemophilia Program to allow for up to three vendors in order to provide the State the flexibility to contract with 1-3 vendors, amend the waiver to include payment in MMA capitation rates for nursing facility services for recipients under the age of 18 years, and amend the waiver to allow flexibility for Specialty Plans that do not have sufficient numbers of eligible members for the mandatory Performance Improvement Projects (PIPs) to conduct PIPs on other topics that have more impact on their members, with the Agency for Health Care Administration’s (Agency) approval.Public Notice and Public Comment: January 12, 2016 -February 11, 2016 The Agency will provide a 30-day public notice and comment period prior to the submission of the waiver amendment to Federal Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS). The Agency will consider all public comments received regarding the development of the waiver amendment.The 30-day public notice and public comment period will be held January 12, 2016 through February 11, 2016.
