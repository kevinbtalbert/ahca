May 27, 2016 –  Public Comment Period on Request to Renew Florida’s Long-term Care Waiver
The State has  made the following changes in the renewal application which differ from the  original approved waiver:

revise       the case management provider qualifications;
revise       the performance measures; 
update       the spousal impoverishment policy;
update       the waiver’s personal needs allowance description;
update       the requirements related to physical therapy;
remove       the structured family caregiving service;  
update       the waiver specific home and community-based settings transition plan; and
update       the waiver’s unduplicated enrollee count and related waiver cost       projections.

Here is the link  to the draft waiver renewal:

A paper copy of  the waiver amendment may be requested by contacting us by mail or e-mail;  please follow the directions outlined below. 
The public may submit comments on the renewal through May 27, 2016.
When providing  comments, please reference “Long-term Care Managed Care Waiver Renewal” in the  subject line.
Comments and  suggestions can be mailed to:  Agency for Health Care Administration  Attention: HCBS Waivers  2727 Mahan Drive, MS #20Tallahassee, Florida 32308
Comments and  suggestions can be emailed to: FLMedicaidWaivers@ahca.myflorida.com.
