 MMA Quarterly Reports 
The Agency is required to submit a quarterly progress report to the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) no later than 60 days following the end of the each quarter as specified in Special Term and Condition #84 of the approved 1115 MMA Waiver.  The quarterly reports are provided below. Each report provides a detailed summary of the events that occurred during the quarter being reported.

Please note: The waiver amendment  approved by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services on June 14, 2013 changed  the name of the waiver name from “Medicaid Reform” to the “Managed Medical  Assistance Waiver”.
