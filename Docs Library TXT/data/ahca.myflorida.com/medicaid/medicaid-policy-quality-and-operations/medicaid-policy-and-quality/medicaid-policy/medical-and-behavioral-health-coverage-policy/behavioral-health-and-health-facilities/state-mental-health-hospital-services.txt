State Mental Health Hospital Services












Florida Medicaid’s Covered Services and HCBS Waivers
State Mental Health  Hospital Services
Medicaid reimburse for  state mental health hospital services that provide room and board, and  long-term inpatient mental health services which include the following  services:

Clinical therapy
Incontinence supplies
Medical
Medical equipment
Nutritional
Personal care
Pharmaceutical
Psychiatric
Rehabilitation, restoration, and recovery

This service is not one of the minimum covered  services for all health plans serving Medicaid enrollees.
Eligibility
Florida Medicaid recipients  requiring medically necessary mental health state hospital services who meet the  following criteria:

Are age 65 years and  older
Meet the Medicaid  Institutional Care Program eligibility requirements
Meet state mental  health hospital level of care criteria

Have a written order for state mental health hospital services from a  licensed doctor of medicine or osteopathy with specialized training and  experience in the diagnosis and treatment of mental illness in accordance with  coverage and limitation requirements
Resource Information
The Florida Medicaid coverage policies, fee schedules, and Rule are  available on the Agency  Website.
