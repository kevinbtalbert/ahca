Florida Medicaid’s Covered Services and HCBS Waivers
Specialized Therapeutic  Foster Care Services
Medicaid reimburses for specialized therapeutic foster care  services to enable a recipient to manage and to work toward resolution of  emotional, behavioral, or psychiatric problems in a highly supportive,  individualized, and flexible home setting. 
Medicaid specialized therapeutic foster care services are  intensive treatment services provided to recipients under the age of 21 years  with emotional disturbances who reside in a state licensed foster home.
This is one of the minimum covered services for all Managed Medical  Assistance plans serving Medicaid enrollees.
Eligibility
Florida Medicaid recipients requiring medically  necessary specialized therapeutic foster care services who meet the following  criteria:

Under the age of  21 years
Must be determined by a multidisciplinary team  to meet criteria in accordance with coverage and limitations requirements

Resource Information 
Information on Medicaid health plans and services is  available on the Statewide  Medicaid Managed Care webpage.
The Florida Medicaid coverage policies, fee schedules, and Rule are  available on the Agency  Website.
