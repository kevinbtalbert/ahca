Prescribed Pediatric Extended Care (PPEC) Services











Florida Medicaid’s Covered Services and HCBS WaiversPrescribed Pediatric Extended Care (PPEC) ServicesMedicaid reimburses for prescribed pediatric extended care (PPEC) services to provide non-residential short-term, long-term or intermittent medical care to recipients with medically-complex conditions.This service is not one of the minimum covered services for managed care plans serving Medicaid enrollees. EligibilityFlorida Medicaid recipients who meet the following criteria may receive Prescribed Pediatric Extended Care (PPEC) services:Are under the age of 21 years.Have complex medical needsAre medically stablePayments/Co-Pays/OtherMedicaid reimburses fee-for-service providers in accordance with the service-specific fee schedule.Resource InformationThe Florida Medicaid coverage policies, fee schedules, and Rule are available on the Agency Website.
