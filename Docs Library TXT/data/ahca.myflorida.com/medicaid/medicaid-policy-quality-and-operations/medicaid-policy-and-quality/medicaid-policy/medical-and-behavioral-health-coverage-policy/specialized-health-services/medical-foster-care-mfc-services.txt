Medical Foster Care (MFC) Services











Florida Medicaid’s Covered Services and HCBS WaiversMedical Foster Care (MFC) ServicesMedical Foster Care services provide recipients with continuous medical care in foster homes.This service is not one of the minimum covered services for managed care plans serving Medicaid enrollees. EligibilityFlorida Medicaid recipients under the age of 21 years requiring medically necessary MFC services who meet the following criteria:Able to have his or her health, safety, and well-being maintained in a foster homeAre determined medically stable by a physicianIn the custody of the Department of Children & Families (DCF), in a voluntary placement agreement, or in extended foster care, in accordance with section 409.175, F.S.Resource InformationThe Florida Medicaid coverage policies, fee schedules, and Rule are available on the Agency Website.Information on the Foster Care program is available on the Department of Children and Families webpage.
