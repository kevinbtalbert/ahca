Federal Qualified Health Center Services











Florida Medicaid’s Covered Services and HCBS WaiversFederal Qualified Health Center ServicesMedicaid reimburses for ambulatory primary care health care and related diagnostic services to a medically underserved population.Medicaid reimburses up to three encounters per day, per recipient for the following:Adult health screeningsBehavioral healthChild Health Check-Up screeningsChiropracticDentalFamily planningImmunizationsMedical primary carePrenatal care and obstetric careOptometricPodiatryRegistered nurse servicesThis service is one of the minimum covered services for all Managed Medical Assistance plans serving Medicaid enrollees.EligibilityAll Medicaid recipients may receive medically necessary federally qualified health center services.Resource InformationInformation on Medicaid health plans and services is available on the Statewide Medicaid Managed Care webpage.The Florida Medicaid coverage policies, fee schedules, and Rule are available on theAgency Website.
