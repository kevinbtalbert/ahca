Evaluation and Management Services











Florida Medicaid’s Covered Services and HCBS WaiversEvaluation and Management ServicesMedicaid reimburses for evaluation and management services to provide for qualified practitioner visits to maintain a recipient’s health, prevent disease, and treat illness.Medicaid reimburses for evaluation and management services including:Adult Health Screening ServicesChild Health Check-Up ServicesCustodial Care Facility Services and Nursing Facility ServicesOffice VisitsEarly and Periodic Screening, Diagnosis, and TreatmentOne adult health screening every 365 days, for recipients age 21 years and olderPreventative medicine services for recipients under the age of 21 yearsOne evaluation and management visit per month, per recipientAs medically necessary for recipients under the age of 21 years and pregnant recipients age 21 years and olderUp to two office visits per month, per specialty, for recipients age 21 years and olderThis service is one of the minimum covered services for all Managed Medical Assistance plans serving Medicaid enrollees.EligibilityFlorida Medicaid recipients requiring medically necessary evaluation and management services.Resource InformationInformation on Medicaid health plans and services is available on the Statewide Medicaid Managed Care webpage.The Florida Medicaid coverage policies, fee schedules, and Rule are available on the Agency Website.
