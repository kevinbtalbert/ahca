Florida Medicaid’s Covered Services and HCBS Waivers
Intermediate Care Facility for Individuals with Intellectual Disabilities  (ICF/IID) Services
Medicaid reimburses for all-inclusive rehabilitative  services for recipients with intellectual disabilities who reside in an  intermediate care facility.Intermediate Care Facility Services includes the following: 

Activity  services
Dental  services
Dietary  services (including therapeutic diet)
Nursing  services
Pharmacy  services
Physician  services
Rehabilitative  care (including physical, speech, occupational and mental health therapies)
Room/  bed and maintenance services
Routine  personal hygiene items
Social  services

This service is not one of the minimum covered services for health plans  serving Medicaid enrollees.
Eligibility
Florida Medicaid recipients  requiring medically necessary ICF/IID services who:

Have the level of  need and level of reimbursement determined by the Agency for Persons with Disabilities  in the last six months
Meet the requirements for the Institutional Care Program

Resource Information 
The Florida Medicaid coverage policies, fee schedules, and Rule are  available on the Agency  Website.
