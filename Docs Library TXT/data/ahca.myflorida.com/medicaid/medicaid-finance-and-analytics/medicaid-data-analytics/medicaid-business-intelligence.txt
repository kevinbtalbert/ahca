Medicaid Business Intelligence
This unit performs statistical analyses to evaluate the  Statewide Medicaid Managed Care program, and communicates results to  stakeholders and the public via publication of the Quarterly Statewide Medicaid  Managed Care Report.  The unit will play a key role in answering the  question: “What are we getting for our money?”
Unit Responsibilities include:

Monitors, evaluates, and reports on plan  performance for the Statewide Medicaid Managed Care program. 
Analyzes enrollment, claims, and encounter  data. 
Assesses the cost effectiveness of the SMMC  program.
Evaluates the quality of provider networks.
Analyzes potentially preventable health care events.
Develops economic and statistical models to  measure healthcare quality and costs.  
Develops metrics and benchmarks for healthcare  analyses.
Reviews and implements current predictive models  and statistical tools for health risk-based analyses and analyses of health  plan performance.
Publishes the Quarterly Statewide Medicaid  Managed Care Report.
