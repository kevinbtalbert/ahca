Medicaid Monthly Enrollment Report











Florida Medicaid Monthly Enrollment ReportThe Agency publishes 16 Medicaid enrollment reports on a monthly basis.  These monthly reports reflect enrollment as of the last day of the reporting month, e.g., September’s report is as of the last day of September. The report is published in excel and has the following 16 worksheets:“MEDICAID”  -  Statewide Medicaid Enrollment Report Official Medicaid Enrollment per month for current reporting month and each preceding month since July 2015, grouped by program and by plan. “MARKETSHARE” - Medicaid SMMC Market Share Report Latest market share for each health plan by program and by plan.“AGEGROUP” - Medicaid Enrollment by AGEGROUP MMA and FFS Enrollment by age group and by plan.“MAN-VOL” - Medicaid Enrollment By Mandatory/Voluntary MMA and FFS Enrollment by Mandatory, Voluntary, age, and plan.“REGION” - Program Enrollment by District Medicaid enrollment by county, region, and program.“REGION_TANF_SSI” – Medicaid Program Enrollment by District by Eligibility Medicaid enrollment by county, region, program, and eligibility.“COUNTY-MMA” - SMMC MMA Enrollment by County, by Plan MMA enrollment by plan and by county.“COUNTY-LTC” - SMMC LTC Enrollment by County by Plan LTC enrollment by plan and by county.“MMAC” - SMMC MMA Capitated Enrollment Report MMA Enrollment by plan, region, and eligibility.“MMASC” - SMMC MMA Specialty Capitated Enrollment Report MMA Specialty Enrollment by plan, region, and eligibility.“MMACC” - SMMC MMA Child Welfare Capitated Enrollment Report Medicaid MMA Child Welfare Enrollment plan, region, and age group.“LTC” - SMMC Long Term Care Capitated Enrollment Report LTC enrollment by plan, region, age group, and eligibility.“LTC_ENR_MMASVC” - SMMC Long Term Care Enrollment for MMA Services MMA and FFS enrollment of LTC enrollees by LTC plan, region, and MMA plan.“CMSMA” - SMMC CMSMA Assignment Enrollment Report CMSMA enrollment by region, age group, and eligibility.“MMAC_MMASC_TANF” - SMMC MMA Age Band Enrollment Report - TANF TANF Eligible by program by MMA plan, region, and age group.  “MMAC_MMASC_SSI” - SMMC MMA Age Band Enrollment Report- SSI and LTC SSI and LTC Eligibility by MMA plan, region, and age group.“SMMC_Dental” Dental enrollment by region by Plan.If you have any questions about this enrollment report, please send an email to the below address:MEDICAIDANALYTICSGENERAL@ahca.myflorida.com  (Please only send clarification questions)Current Comprehensive Medicaid Managed Care Enrollment Reports2024

Child Health Check-Up DataSection 409.912[28), Florida Statutes, requires the Agency to publish annually the Child Health Check-Up (CHCUP) screening rates of each health plan it contracts with on a prepaid basis to serve Medicaid recipients. Each health plan is required to achieve an annual CHCUP screening rate of at least 60 percent for those Medicaid recipients between the ages of less than 1 year to less than 21 years who are enrolled continuously in the same health plan for at least 8 months.Beginning October 1, 2014, the Florida Screening Ratio requirement was increased to 80%.
