July 2021 Amendment - Developmental Disabilities Individual Budgeting (iBudget) Waiver
iBudget Waiver Amendment
The  Agency for Health Care Administration (Agency) is announcing the start of a  30-day public notice and comment period.  The Agency is seeking to amend  the Developmental Disabilities Individual Budgeting Waiver (FL.0867), which  operates under the authority of Section 1915(c) of the Social Security  Act.  The 30-day public notice and comment period will be held from January 13, 2021 through February 11, 2021, prior to the submission of the amendment request to the  Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services.
Changes  to the approved waiver that are being made in the amendment application  include:

An update to APD’s contact information. 
An update to the qualifications of individuals who  perform reevaluations
Removal of individuals as a provider type option for  support coordination
An update to support coordination agency requirements
An update to the waiver’s qualified providers Performance  Measures
Removal of reference to iBudget system and replace with  APD iConnect

When  providing comments, please include “Proposed Amendment to  1915(c) iBudget Waiver” in  the subject line:

Submit  email comments to
          FLMedicaidWaivers@ahca.myflorida.com
        .
Submit  comments by mail to Agency for Health Care Administration, Bureau of Medicaid  Policy, 2727 Mahan Drive, MS 20, Tallahassee, Florida 32308.
