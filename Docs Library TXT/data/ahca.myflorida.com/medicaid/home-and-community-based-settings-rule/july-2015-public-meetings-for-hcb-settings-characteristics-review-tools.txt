July 2015 – Public Meetings for HCB Settings Characteristics  Review Tools
The public meetings will give stakeholders a general overview of  the federal rule and introduce the draft HCB Characteristics Review  Tool–Residential Settings and the HCB Characteristics Review  Tool–Non-Residential Settings for public input. When finalized, providers will  be able to use these tools to self-assess their settings in order to be  compliant with the HCB Settings Rule.
The meetings held  8:30 a.m. – 12:30 p.m. will be open to  all stakeholders and dedicated to the Long-Term Care (LTC) Waiver, Project AIDS  Care (PAC) Waiver, and Traumatic Brain Injury/Spinal Cord Injury (TBI/SCI) Waiver.  The meetings held  1:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m. will be open to all stakeholders  and dedicated to the Developmental Disabilities Individual Budgeting (iBudget)  Waiver. The dates, times and locations of the public meetings are provided  under the Schedule of Public Meetings heading. The draft HCB Settings Characteristics Review Tool documents  are available for review below.
