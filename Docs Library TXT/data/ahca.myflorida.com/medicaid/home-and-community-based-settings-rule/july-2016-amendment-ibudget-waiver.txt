July 2016  Amendment - iBudget Waiver
The  Agency is requesting an amendment of the Individual Budgeting (iBudget) Waiver to:

Define  and add individuals diagnosed with Phelan McDermid syndrome as an eligible  population for participation on the waiver (Appendix B)
Increase  the unduplicated numbers for:
Remove  Transitional Living Facilities from the list of qualified providers of  residential habilitation (Appendix C)
Add  Comprehensive Transitional Education Program as a qualified provider of  residential habilitation services (Appendix C)
Remove  in-home support from the residential habilitation service taxonomy (Appendix C)
Update  safeguards for restraints and restrictive interventions (Appendix G)
Update  the medication management and administration section in the following areas:  responsibilities, state oversight and follow-up, state policy, and medication  error reporting (Appendix G) 
Update J-Table estimates for waiver years three  through five, and increase the rates for the following services: respite,  personal supports, life skills development-level 3, and residential  habilitation (Appendix J)
Update milestones related to the implementation of the  state’s Home and Community-Based Services Settings’ Rule Transition Plan
Year 3 to 35,218
Year 4 to 38,018
Year 5 to 40,818  (Appendix B)
