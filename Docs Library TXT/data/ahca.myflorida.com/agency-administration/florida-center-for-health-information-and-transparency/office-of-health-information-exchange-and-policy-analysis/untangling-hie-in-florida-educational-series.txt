Untangling HIE in Florida Educational Series



















 



 WHAT IS UNTANGLING HIE IN FLORIDA?Untangling HIE in Florida is an educational series that aims to address the complexities within Health Information Exchange (HIE). The world of HIE contains a wide range of activities- solutions, regulations, and an everchanging ecosystem to go with it. All of these different aspects can cause confusion to those exploring or new to the industry. The Agency for Health Care Administration's Office of Health Information Exchange & Policy Analysis has taken it upon ourselves to invite experts from the field to discuss what is going on in the space from national data exchange to state reporting.HOW OFTEN IS UNTANGLING HIE IN FLORIDA HELD?Sessions are held twice monthly.HOW CAN I BE NOTIFIED WHEN A SESSION IS BEING HELD?You can either visit this webpage to find upcoming sessions or if you would like to be informed via email invite, contact FLHII@ahca.myflorida.comARE SESSIONS RECORDED?Yes.
