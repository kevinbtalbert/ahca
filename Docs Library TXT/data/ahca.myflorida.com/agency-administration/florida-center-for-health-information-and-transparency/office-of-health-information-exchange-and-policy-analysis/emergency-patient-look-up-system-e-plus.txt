Emergency Patient Look-Up System (E-PLUS)















E-PLUS is a HIPAA compliant, cloud-based software solution designed to support public health officials and emergency responders to fill information gaps during disaster response. E-PLUS is made available by the Agency for Health Care Administration (AHCA) at no cost and enables authorized users to access medication and clinical histories, search for missing persons, and alert providers that their patients have been evacuated to Special Needs Shelters.E-PLUS supports disaster response activities through the following applications:
