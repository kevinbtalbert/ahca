Committees and Councils











This section provides information on various committees, including member information, scheduled meetings and events, and meeting documents.State Consumer Health Information and Policy Advisory Council assists the Agency with the identification, collection, standardization, sharing, and coordination of health-related data, among federal, state, local, and private entities and recommends improvements for purposes of public health, policy analysis, health information exchange and transparency of consumer health care information.Health Information Exchange Coordinating Committee provides guidance to the Agency as it develops and implements specific programs for the creation of a statewide health information exchange network, the adoption of electronic medical record systems, and ensuring the privacy and security of health information.Health Information Exchange Legal Work Group meets to discuss general topics relating to legal policies for health information exchange including provisions of a participation agreement and other subscription agreements for health information exchange.
